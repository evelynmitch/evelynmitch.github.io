Event Details

Organizer: Explore Mars, Inc.

Explore Mars, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation created to advance the goal of sending humans to Mars within the next two decades. To further that goal, Explore Mars, Inc. conducts programs and technical challenges to stimulate the development and/or improvement of technologies that will make human Mars missions more efficient and feasible. In addition, to embed the idea of Mars as a habitable planet, Explore Mars, Inc. challenges educators to use Mars in the classroom as a tool to teach standard STEM curricula.

The Space Exploration Alliance (SEA) will be holding its annual grassroots visit to Congress, known as the "Legislative Blitz", in Washington, D.C. from Sunday, February 24 to Tuesday, February 26, 2019.

There will be a mandatory information/training session for all registered "blitzers" on the afternoon of Sunday, February 24th (from 1:00 p.m. to approximately 5:30 p.m.), in preparation for meetings on Monday, February 25th and Tuesday, February 26th.

The Space Exploration Alliance is a collaboration of leading non-profit organizations that advocate for the exploration and development of outer space, including Explore Mars, Inc., Federation of Galaxy Explorers, Moon Society, The Mars Society, National Society of Black Engineers, National Space Society, Buzz Aldrin's ShareSpace Foundation, and Students for the Exploration and Development of Space.

(Please note that registration for this event closes on January 31, 2019 or when we reach capacity, whichever comes first. Due to scheduling requirements, we regret that we will be unable to accommodate any requests to register after that date.)
